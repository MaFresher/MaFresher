    Substance abuse-is the use of illegal drugs or the use of prescription or over the counter drugs or alcohol for purposes other than those for which they are meant to be used, or in excessive amounts. On this issue you will be addresed on different types of substances,how they affect the lifes of the people(health awereness) ,how can you get help if you find yourself in substances and realise the damages they cause in you body or life in general and what can the community do to stop the use/intake of substances .

    Gender-based violence (GBV)-refers to harmful acts directed at an individual based on their gender. It is rooted in gender inequality, the abuse of power and harmful norms. It is a serious violation of human rights and a life-threatening health and protection issue. Gender-based violence can include sexual, physical, mental and economic harm perpetrated in public or in private. It also includes threats of violence, intimidation, and manipulation. This can take many forms such as intimate partner violence, sexual violence, child marriage.On this issue you will be addresed on how it affect the lifes of the people in many ways, how can you get help if you find yourself as a victim or someone acting upon the rights of another and on how / what can the community do to help such people.

    Unemployment-is a term referring to individuals who are employable and actively seeking a job but are unable to find a job. Included in this group are those people in the workforce who are working but do not have an appropriate job. Unemployment serves as one of the huge problems that communities are facing.On this issue you will be informed on how and what to do if you are unmployed.What can the community do to help people who are unemployed.
